21 Nov 2018 | Khanyiso "Dr West" Haman

Technology is has been a big thing since the 18th century, it has been part of our lives since ages ago. It has been constantly developing and being refined, new ideas has came up, innovations has been created but has technology changed us for the better or for the worse. Many have agreed to disagree, like many things in this realm technology has its advantages and disadvantages.

When it comes to the way we communicate, modern technology has had an impressive influence on communication in the 21st century and daily life in general.In the past, you had to write a letter to communicate with someone. The letter could even take days before it was delivered. You also had to trace the letter to see if it had been sent to and received by the appropriate person. Errors were not uncommon as well.

Very few students and teachers would disagree with this notion. The advancement in technology has made it possible for teachers to impart knowledge to students from any location.
